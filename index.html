Chicken a la King

This is a delicious creamy chicken recipe that is great with rice or as a noodle dish.

Ingredients

  • 500g of cubed chicken
  • 250g of chopped bell peppers
  • 1 pack of mushroom soup
  • 500ml of milk
  • salt, pepper, aromat, paprika, basil

Instructions

  1. Coat the chicken cubes in the spices to taste.
  2. Fry until golden brown.
  3. Add chopped bell peppers and cook for around 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add the whole pack of mushroom soup and mix through.
  5. Add the milk and stir consistently until it thickens.
  6. If the soup is too thick, add more milk. If it's too thin, add more soup powder.
  7. Cook rice on the side and serve together.
